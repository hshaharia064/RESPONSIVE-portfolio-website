About
Iron Man, also known as Tony Stark, is a fictional superhero in the Marvel Universe, created by writer Stan Lee, developed by scripter Larry Lieber, and designed by artists Don Heck and Jack Kirby. First appearing in Tales of Suspense #39 in 1963, Iron Man is the alter ego of Tony Stark, a billionaire industrialist, genius inventor, and playboy. Starkâ€™s life changes when he is kidnapped and critically injured. To survive, he builds a powered suit of armor, which also helps him escape captivity. Upon returning home, Stark refines his suit, becoming the armored superhero known as Iron Man. Iron Man's suit is equipped with advanced technology, including flight capabilities, enhanced strength, and a variety of weapons, such as repulsor beams. Tony Stark is also notable for his sharp wit, intelligence, and leadership, playing a pivotal role in founding the Avengers. In the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU), Iron Man is portrayed by Robert Downey Jr., becoming one of the most iconic characters in the franchise. His journey spans multiple films, culminating in his heroic sacrifice in Avengers: Endgame, where he saves the universe from Thanos' forces. Iron Man's legacy is marked by his intelligence, bravery, and transformative redemption story.
